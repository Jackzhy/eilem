Our Features

SECURITY

In decentralized trading platform, every transaction is process through ethereum smart contract (ERC20). This allow point to point transaction to be accomplished in a highly secured form. With Eilem, it is needless for user to create and stored any assets to use our platform since each transaction is traded through user’s personal wallet (MetaMask, EtherWallet, etc).

CUSTOMER TO CUSTOMER

Eilem, as a C2C digital asset trading platform, is well aware of the importance of individual merchants. We strongly encourage and are willing to help our customers to build their own business on our platform. Since most of people are lack of adequate knowledge for this industry, we will support them with our well-training team. We believe that building a close relationship with customer like this would be a stepping stone to future success.

SERVICE

Since the market of blockchain just started growing, most of the existing services or products are unsatisfactory to meet the people’s growing need. Eilem, as a first-mover in this field, aims to solve an urgent need of high-quality services. For each merchant, we provide them a set of services, including advisory and sale of on-chain asset, the consulting service of growing their own business. For every customer, with our global training team, we would serve them as perfect as traditional service industry do.

  • Seamless transactions

    Our platform try to bring the users a most simple and elegant trading experience. Every transaction will be executed by smart contract on blockchain in real-time. The users have no needs to deposit or withdraw any assets. The assets are in users wallets right after the transactions are executed. The users even don’t have to register, sign in or sign out to trade, if they want.

  • Diverse assets support

    We provide not only the trade in digital currency but also other digital assets. Under the developing tendency of blockchain, digital assets has already become an important part of the future trading methods. For now, we are trying to construct a trading platform for diverse virtual assets such as ens domain and any blockchain applications. Along with the rapid progress of blockchain technology, we believe the physical assets such as real estate and car will be transform into digital asset through on-chain process. Then, we will support the trading of those kind of properties as well.
